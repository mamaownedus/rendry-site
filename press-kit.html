Press Kit

One-liner

One-click full-page screenshots — private, clean, and fast.

Short pitch (25 words)

Rendry is a Chrome extension that captures entire web pages as PNG or PDF. Privacy-first — everything stays in your browser. No account, no tracking.

Long pitch (75 words)

Rendry is a full-page screenshot Chrome extension built for people who don't want their screenshots leaving their machine. It captures entire pages — including lazy-loaded images — as PNG or PDF, automatically hides cookie banners and chat widgets before the shot, and handles sticky headers without repetition. The free tier makes zero network requests. An optional Pro tier ($2/month) adds JPEG/WebP export, a built-in annotation editor, and clipboard copy.

Key facts

  • Price: Free. Optional Pro at $2/month.
  • Platform: Chrome extension, Manifest V3.
  • Publisher: MamaOwned LLC (United States).
  • Privacy: Zero network requests in the free tier. No tracking, no analytics, no cookies.
  • Permissions: activeTab, scripting, downloads, storage, offscreen, clipboardWrite. No host permissions.
