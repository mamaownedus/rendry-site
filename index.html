FAQ

Common questions.

Is Rendry really free?

Yes. The free tier captures full pages as PNG or PDF, handles lazy-loaded images, removes popups, and detects infinite scroll — all without any server calls. Pro ($2/month) adds extra formats and an annotation editor.

Does Rendry track me?

No. The free tier makes zero network requests and has no analytics, telemetry, or cookies. If you upgrade to Pro, ExtensionPay receives your email and subscription status only — never browsing data or screenshots.

Does Rendry work offline?

Yes. The capture pipeline (scroll, capture, stitch, export) runs entirely in your browser with no server dependency. The only network calls are license checks for Pro users.

Can I use Rendry on any website?

Yes. Rendry uses the activeTab permission, which grants access only when you click the extension. It works on any standard web page — including sites with lazy-loaded images, infinite scroll, and sticky headers.

What's the refund policy?

7-day no-questions refund on your first subscription. After that, cancellations stop the next renewal but the current paid month isn't refunded. See the full refund policy.

How do I report a bug or request a feature?

Open an issue at github.com/mamaownedus/rendry-site/issues. For usage questions, check Discussions.

Is Rendry open source?

The landing page and issue tracker are public. The extension source is currently private to keep the billing integration straightforward. That may change — let us know if it matters to you.
